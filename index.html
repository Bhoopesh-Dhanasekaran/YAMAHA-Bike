The Fastest YAMAHA Bike - Yamaha YZF R1

The Yamaha YZF R1 998cc inline-four-cylinder engine features Yamaha’s exclusive crossplane crankshaft technology derived from the Yamaha YZF R1 MotoGP bike. Every feature of this unique engine is made to thrill.

The Yamaha YZF R1 super sport bike features a cable-less ride-by-wire YCC-T (Yamaha Chip Controlled Throttle) system that transforms the rider’s inputs into motion, with a full suite of IMU-powered electronic rider aids that make new meaning to the term “biker confidence.”

The YZF-R1 sportbike uses track-focused braking hardware, featuring stainless steel front lines and big 320mm rotors high-friction pads, potent 4-piston radial-mounted front calipers and a compact ABS unit. The Bridgestone® RS11 tires ensure right racetrack-ready traction with flat road feel and handling.

The Yamaha YZF-R1 bike features sleek bodywork to build an aerodynamic profile, with an integrated and friendly design.

Built around Yamaha’s refined Deltabox aluminum frame technology and fully-adjustable suspension, mounting high-spec, the Yamaha YZF-R1 bike exemplifies over 20 years of chassis technology developed on paths around the world.
